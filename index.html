Vehicle scrapping refers to the process of dismantling and disposing of old or end-of-life vehicles. It involves recycling and extracting valuable materials from the vehicles while ensuring that harmful substances are properly disposed of or treated.

In response to the escalating pollution and traffic levels in India, government agencies have enacted a new regulation mandating the disposal of End-of-Life (ELV) vehicles, recognizing their significant contribution to environmental contamination. Consequently, vehicles exceeding 15 years of age (10 years for diesel) must undergo scrapping.

Sri RVSF acts as your liaison, linking you with authorized vehicle scrapping facilities in close proximity. Rest assured, our services encompass comprehensive legal documentation and the diligent execution of scrapping procedures, guaranteeing the proper disposal of your car.
