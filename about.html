About

Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facility (RVSF) ensures safe and environmentally friendly disposal of vehicles. Its primary goal is to promote a legally compliant dismantling and scrapping industry. RVSFs handle end-of-life vehicles responsibly, following regulations for proper disposal of hazardous materials, recycling of reusable components, and environmentally sound treatment of vehicle fluids.
